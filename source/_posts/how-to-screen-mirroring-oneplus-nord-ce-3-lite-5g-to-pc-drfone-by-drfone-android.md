Adb shell

ifconfig

A list shall appear on the Command Prompt, showing a list of all the local IP addresses associated with the computer. You need to locate the IP address of your OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G Android phone and have it copied on the clipboard.

**Step 4: Close and Re-Type IP Address**
