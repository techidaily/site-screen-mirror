## Method 1: Share Your Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max to a TV or Mac Using AirPlay

The first method that you can use to share your Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max screen with another device is AirPlay. ### What Are the Requirements?

- For iPhones/iPad users, iOS 14 and later versions are suitable, while Mac users can activate this function on macOS Monterey or newer.
- To allow some of the Siri features to operate while operating AirPlay to **screen share iPhone devices,** you should get HomeKit first.

**Step 1:** To screen share your Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max to a TV, you must connect both devices to the same Wi-Fi network. Open the video that needs to be shared on the TV.

**Step 2:** Look for the "Share" button on the video and tap the "AirPlay" option to continue connecting your Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max to the Apple TV. You must select your TV after tapping on the "AirPlay" option.
