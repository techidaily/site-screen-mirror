- It is possible that the Apple iPhone 8 Plus devices in use for screencasting are not compatible. For that, you must check the Apple iPhone 8 Plus devices to figure out if they are compatible and supports AirPlay.

- It is possible that the AirPlay connectivity is hindered between iPhone and Mac due to Firewall Settings. Therefore, you will have to disable and allow some settings for that purpose.
