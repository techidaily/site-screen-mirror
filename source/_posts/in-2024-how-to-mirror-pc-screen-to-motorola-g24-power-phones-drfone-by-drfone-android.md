2\. Tap on the Settings menu of your Android device;

3\. Go to Additional Settings and tap on Developer Options;

5\. You will see the Build Number of the Motorola G24 Power device. Tap on the option 7 times. It will bring the Motorola G24 Power device into developer mode;

6\. Repeat Step 2!

7\. Enable USB Debugging and tap on OK to grant permission.
