- It is possible that the Apple iPhone 7 devices in use for screencasting are not compatible. For that, you must check the Apple iPhone 7 devices to figure out if they are compatible and supports AirPlay.
- As the connection is established through a Wi-Fi network, you must check whether the Apple iPhone 7 devices are connected to the same network. In other cases, it is also possible that the Wi-Fi connection is not strong enough.
