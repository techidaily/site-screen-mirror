On to Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max, screen mirroring can be done using Lightning to HDMI Adapter or Lightning to VGA Adapter. For Wired connection, simply follow the given steps:

2) Connect the lightning end of the adapter to the to Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max.

3) Change TV /PC to HDMI or VGA input and hence, to Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max screen is being mirrored on TV /PC.
