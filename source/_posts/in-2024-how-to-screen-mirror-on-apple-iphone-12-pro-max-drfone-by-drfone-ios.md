## Part 1. How to screen mirror on Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max wirelessly? - Airplay

To screen mirror on Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max wirelessly you will need an [Apple TV](https://drfone.wondershare.com/mirror-emulator/airplay-without-apple-tv.html) that makes it compatible with Airplay. Just follow the simple steps below and enjoy a large screen display within minutes.

1\. Make sure to connect your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max and TV to be on the same network.

2\. Unlock your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max and play the video you want to enjoy.

3\. Swipe up to reach the Control Centre of your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max.

4\. Switch on the AirPlay.

5\. Select the "Screen Mirroring" option from Control Centre.

8\. Tap on the play button so that TV will give you the display of your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max screen.
