5\. You will see the Build Number of the Motorola Moto G73 5G device. Tap on the option 7 times. It will bring the Motorola Moto G73 5G device into developer mode;

6\. Repeat Step 2!

7\. Enable USB Debugging and tap on OK to grant permission.
