- It is possible that the Apple iPhone 7 devices in use for screencasting are not compatible. For that, you must check the Apple iPhone 7 devices to figure out if they are compatible and supports AirPlay.

- Your Apple iPhone 7 or Mac can cause issues with connectivity through AirPlay if they are not updated to the latest version.
- It is possible that the AirPlay connectivity is hindered between iPhone and Mac due to Firewall Settings. Therefore, you will have to disable and allow some settings for that purpose.
