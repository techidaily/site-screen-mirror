## Part 1:What are Android Miracast and Apple Airplay

****Android Miracast:****

Sometimes, you may wonder **whats miracast**.The Wi-Fi Alliance created the wireless display standard known as Miracast. It is a technique that permits wireless display from one device to another, from a smartphone or tablet to a TV or monitor, without the need for cables or a Wi-Fi network. This technology is not particular to Android devices.

Miracast can be utilized with various devices, including smartphones, tablets, computers, and TVs. It employs Wi-Fi Direct technology to establish a peer-to-peer connection between devices. It cannot be used for ****Apple iPhone XS casting****.

****Supported Devices:****

- For smartphones and tablets, many Android devices support Miracast, including Samsung Galaxy phones, LG phones, and Sony Xperia phones. However, Miracast is not officially supported on Apple iPhone XSs or iPads.
