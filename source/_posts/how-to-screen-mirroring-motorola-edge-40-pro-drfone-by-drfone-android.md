Screen mirroring Motorola Edge 40 Pro to any TV or PC is easy to be carried out. Screen mirroring in Motorola Edge 40 Pro functions by enabling wireless display function. Follow the given simple steps and your smartphone screen will be cast to a large screen.

1\. Enable wifi on both devices.

2\. If you are using a PC go to setting, enable the projecting screen.

3\. For TV see TV’s manual for enabling screencast features.

4\. On your Motorola Edge 40 Pro, go to Settings> Network> More> Wireless Display.

5\. Enable wireless display; it will automatically scan the wireless display devices.

6\. You can connect either PC or TV you want to by selecting that device.
