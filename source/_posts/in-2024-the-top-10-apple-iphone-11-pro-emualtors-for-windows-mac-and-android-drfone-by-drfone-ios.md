Just follow the simple steps below and enjoy a large screen display within minutes.

1\. Make sure to connect your Apple iPhone 11 Pro and TV to be on the same network.

2\. Unlock your Apple iPhone 11 Pro and play the video you want to enjoy.

3\. Swipe up to reach the Control Centre of your Apple iPhone 11 Pro.

4\. Switch on the AirPlay.

5\. Select the "Screen Mirroring" option from Control Centre.
