## Part 1. How to screen mirror on Apple iPhone 14 wirelessly? - Airplay

To screen mirror on Apple iPhone 14 wirelessly you will need an [Apple TV](https://drfone.wondershare.com/mirror-emulator/airplay-without-apple-tv.html) that makes it compatible with Airplay. Airplay has been devised by Apple to make it easy to stream videos on a large screen from your handset. Your Apple iPhone 14 and Apple TV should be on the same network for this purpose. Just follow the simple steps below and enjoy a large screen display within minutes.

1\. Make sure to connect your Apple iPhone 14 and TV to be on the same network.

2\. Unlock your Apple iPhone 14 and play the video you want to enjoy.

3\. Swipe up to reach the Control Centre of your Apple iPhone 14.

4\. Switch on the AirPlay.

5\. Select the "Screen Mirroring" option from Control Centre.

![screen mirror on iphone 8 1](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2020/08/screen-mirror-on-iphone-8-1.jpg)

6\. Select your device i.e. Apple TV from scanned devices.

![screen mirror on iphone 8 2](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2020/08/screen-mirror-on-iphone-8-2.jpg)

7\. Exit the Control Centre.

8\. Tap on the play button so that TV will give you the display of your Apple iPhone 14 screen.
