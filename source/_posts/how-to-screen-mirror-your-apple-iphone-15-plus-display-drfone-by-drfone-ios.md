- It is possible that the Apple iPhone 15 Plus devices in use for screencasting are not compatible. For that, you must check the Apple iPhone 15 Plus devices to figure out if they are compatible and supports AirPlay.
- As the connection is established through a Wi-Fi network, you must check whether the Apple iPhone 15 Plus devices are connected to the same network. In other cases, it is also possible that the Wi-Fi connection is not strong enough.
- Your Apple iPhone 15 Plus or Mac can cause issues with connectivity through AirPlay if they are not updated to the latest version.
- It is possible that the AirPlay connectivity is hindered between iPhone and Mac due to Firewall Settings. Therefore, you will have to disable and allow some settings for that purpose.
