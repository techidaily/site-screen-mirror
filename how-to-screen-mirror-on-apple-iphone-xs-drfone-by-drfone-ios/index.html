Part 1. How to screen mirror on Apple iPhone XS wirelessly? - Airplay

To screen mirror on Apple iPhone XS wirelessly you will need an Apple TV that makes it compatible with Airplay. Airplay has been devised by Apple to make it easy to stream videos on a large screen from your handset. Your Apple iPhone XS and Apple TV should be on the same network for this purpose. Just follow the simple steps below and enjoy a large screen display within minutes.

1. Make sure to connect your Apple iPhone XS and TV to be on the same network.

2. Unlock your Apple iPhone XS and play the video you want to enjoy.

3. Swipe up to reach the Control Centre of your Apple iPhone XS.

4. Switch on the AirPlay.

5. Select the “Screen Mirroring” option from Control Centre.

6. Select your device i.e. Apple TV from scanned devices.

7. Exit the Control Centre.

8. Tap on the play button so that TV will give you the display of your Apple iPhone XS screen.
