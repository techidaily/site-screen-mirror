Part 1: Use HDMI to connect Apple iPhone X to TV

Using HDMI is the simplest way to connect an Apple iPhone X to the TV for screen mirroring and streaming video, playing games etc. This method connects by use a cable that supports the port of the TV and our iPhone. We need a HDMI Adaptor cable called Lightning Digital AV Adaptor . Let’s learn the easy and simple steps:

Step 1. Connect Lightning Digital AV Adaptor to iPhone/iPad

As we know, HDMI adapter plays the most significant role in this method, we have to connect the Digital AV Adaptor to the iPhone or iPad in this step.

Step 2. Connect the Adaptor to TV using HDMI Cable
