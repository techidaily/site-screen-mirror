Sometimes, you may wonder whats miracast.The Wi-Fi Alliance created the wireless display standard known as Miracast. This technology is not particular to Android devices.

Miracast can be utilized with various devices, including smartphones, tablets, computers, and TVs. It employs Wi-Fi Direct technology to establish a peer-to-peer connection between devices. It cannot be used for Apple iPhone SE (2022) casting.

Supported Devices:
