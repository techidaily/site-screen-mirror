Full Guide on Mirroring Your Oppo A78 to Your PC/Mac

1.Why People Want to Mirror Their Android to PC?

The best thing about Android phones these days is that they are like mini computers in which you can save lots of things like photographs, videos, music, and even your important documents. Carrying a phone is very convenient as well, and you have the whole world gathered into a single device. But there are times when you need to show something important to other people on your phone and you need to connect it to your PC, especially if it is some important information which you collected from the internet, and want to show your family or colleagues. In situations like this mirroring, your Android to PC becomes really important as you don’t have to mail or send the data to everyone.

2. Ways in Which You Can Mirror Android to PC

There are many ways through which you can mirror Android to PC different apps are available for this purpose as well. You can mirror the Android to PC by using either your WiFi or your USB port. Both methods are practical and successful.

2.1 Mirror Android to PC with WiFi

2.1.1 MirrorOp Sender

MirrorOp Sender is a device that you can easily use for mirroring your Android with your PC by using your WiFi.

How Does MirrorOp Work:

MirrorOp is available on the PlayStore and can be downloaded easily. Before mirroring your Android with a PC, make sure that your Android is rooted.

  • • Download MirrorOp Sender to your Android.
  • • Download the windows version of the app called MirrorOp Receiver on your PC
  • • Connect the Android and PC to a common WiFi network.
  • • Run the MirrorOp Sender app on your PC.
  • • Run the MirrorOp Receiver app on your Android.
  • • Both the Oppo A78 devices will search each other automatically.
  • • You can now start mirroring.
  • • You can control your Android device through a keyboard and mouse.

2.1.2 Miracast

Miracast is an application that is used for mirroring Android with PC via a WiFi connection.

  • • After installing Miracast from the above-mentioned link on your Android device swipe from the right and select the Oppo A78 devices option.
  • • Select the Project option from there.
  • • An “Add a wireless display” option will appear on your device from which you can select your WiFi connection.
  • • From your PC, you can go to the Settings and click on the Oppo A78 devices tab. By clicking on the “Add a device” option, you can search for the Miracast receiver.
  • • From your device, go to Settings and from there to the Oppo A78 device section and tap on Display. Select Cast Screen from there.
  • • Select the Menu button and tap on Enable Wireless Display. Your device will now search for Miracast devices and display it under the Cast Screen option. Tap on the option and a notification that your screen is being cast will appear.

Now, you can easily mirror your Android with your PC.

2.2 Mirror Android to PC with USB

2.2.1 Android-Screen Monitor

For mirroring Android to PC via a USB, you must got JAVA installed on your PC. On the other hand, the Developer mode should be enabled on your Android device for the successful mirroring of the Oppo A78 device.

Once your requirements are complete, you can download Android-Screen Monitor from https://code.google.com/p/android-screen-monitor/

  • • Download and install JRE or Java Runtime Environment.
  • • Install Android Software Development Kit (SDK) and the associated tools to your PC’s Program Folder.
  • • Once it is installed run the application and select only Android SDK-Platform Tools.
  • • Go to the Settings in your phone or Android device, select Developer Options, and from there go over to the USB Debugging option and enable it.

  • • Look for the drivers associated with your Android device in Google and download it into a separate folder on your PC.
  • • Now you can connect your Oppo A78 to your computer through a USB
  • • Open the Oppo A78 device Manager and look for your Android device.
  • • Now, it’s time to set an ADB path.
  • • Open the Properties of your computer and click on the Advanced System Settings option. Select Environment Variables and look for “Path”.
  • • Once found, click and save edit it to C:Program Files (x86)Androidandroid-SDK platform-tools
  • • Save.

  • • Now, download Android Screen Monitor and install it on your computer.
  • • Now, your computer is mirrored with your android.

2.2.2 Droid@Screen

Droid@Screen is another popular application that is used to mirror Android to PC through a USB.

  • • For using this app, you first need to download JAVA Run Time Application on your PC and install it.
  • • Now, download the ADB tool by extracting it from your desktop.
  • • Download Droid@Screen from the given link and run the application.
  • • Now, click on ADB and select the ADB Executable Path.
  • • Choose the ADB folder to which you had extracted it before and click OK.

  • • In your Android device, open the Settings and go to the Developer Options.
  • • Turn the Developer Options on and select the USB Debugging mode under it.
  • • Connect your device to your PC after installing all the necessary drivers from the internet.
  • • Your device has been mirrored to your PC.

Step 2: Cast Android to Roku:

Part 1. What is TeamViewer?

TeamViewer is a remote access management platform for companies and regular people. They use it for better team activity management, especially remote teams. They can experience better work productivity when using this software, like with the screen-sharing features.

The latter allows for screen-to-screen content broadcasting that many people from varying locations. This is useful for multi-member meetings.

When users operate with TeamViewer to screen-share to PC, there are many advantages they can enjoy. Here are some of them, explained in points for your reference.

  • Improved collaboration- Professionals in different sectors and sizes can collaborate better using the TeamViewer screen-sharing feature. Organizers can share their Android screens with different meeting participants. And then the latter can work on the documents from any location or device.

  • Better onboarding and tutoring- Professionals using TeamViewer can do onboarding and training processes. For example, by sharing the screen with participants, they can visually show how processes or software types work.
