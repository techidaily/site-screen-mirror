To screen mirror on Apple iPhone 15 wirelessly you will need an Apple TV that makes it compatible with Airplay. Airplay has been devised by Apple to make it easy to stream videos on a large screen from your handset. Your Apple iPhone 15 and Apple TV should be on the same network for this purpose. Just follow the simple steps below and enjoy a large screen display within minutes.

5. Select the “Screen Mirroring” option from Control Centre.
